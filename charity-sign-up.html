How It Works For Charities

Our platform revolutionizes fundraising by connecting your organization with crypto traders who want to make a difference. Here's how we empower your mission:

50% of Trading Fees Directed to Causes

When users trade on our platform, half of all transaction fees are automatically directed to verified charitable organizations like yours, creating a steady stream of donations without requiring additional effort from donors.

Verified Charity Status

We thoroughly verify all participating organizations to ensure legitimacy and build trust with our trading community. Your verified status gives traders confidence that their contributions are making a real impact.

Transparent Fund Distribution

Our blockchain-based system provides complete transparency in how funds are collected and distributed. Both your organization and donors can track contributions in real-time, enhancing accountability and trust.

Simplified Crypto Donations

We handle all the technical aspects of cryptocurrency donations, including conversion to traditional currency if preferred. No blockchain expertise is required from your team.

Frequently Asked Questions

Who is eligible to apply?

Registered non-profit organizations, NGOs, and charitable foundations with valid tax-exempt status are eligible to apply. We accept applications from organizations worldwide, though verification requirements may vary by country.

How are donations distributed?

When our platform launches, 50% of all trading fees will be automatically allocated to our pool of verified charities. Funds are distributed monthly, either in cryptocurrency or converted to traditional currency based on your preference.

Is there a cost to join?

No, there is absolutely no cost for charitable organizations to join our platform. We do not charge application fees, membership fees, or take any percentage of the donations you receive.

What verification documents will be needed?

You'll need to provide proof of your organization's registered charitable status, such as an IRS determination letter (for US organizations) or equivalent documentation for international organizations. Additional documentation may be requested during the verification process.

When will the platform launch?

We're currently in the Pre-Seed stage of development and aim to launch in Q1 2026. Organizations on our waitlist will be the first to be onboarded and will receive advance notice before the official launch.

Can we direct funds to specific projects?

Yes, once approved, you'll have the ability to create profiles for different projects or initiatives within your organization. Traders can choose to direct their contributions to specific projects that align with their values.
